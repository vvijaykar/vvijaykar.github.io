Evaluating Benetech

As a part of my Dimensions of Culture (DOC) requirement at UCSD, I wrote a paper looking at a local community organization and assessing a novel solution to their impact model in the class DOC100D "The Promises and Contradictions of U.S. Culture." The organization that appealed to me the most was Benetech, a Bay-area based non-profit that pioneered software for social good. My paper focused on a critical issue facing nonprofit organizations across the country (grant allocation), and provided a custom solution that not only aligned with the vision and mission of Benetech but also the technological scope of their work.
